SYNOPSIS

Synopsis of the short film 'DRACBO'

DRACBO

‘DRACBO’ is a short film centered on the theme of dreams. It tells the story of a passionate teenager who loves photography and is determined to pursue his dreams. He firmly believes in the meaning of chasing dreams, and throughout this journey, he inspires his classmates to run for their passions and strive to prove themselves. Ultimately, he aims to complete a work that belongs to him! The word ‘dream’ is not laughable; what’s laughable are those who only talk and take no real action.
