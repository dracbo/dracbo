SYNOPSIS

Synopsis of the short film 'DRACBO'

DRACBO

‘DRACBO’ is a short film centered around the theme of dreams. It tells the story of a passionate and optimistic young boy who loves programming and is determined to pursue his dream. Along the way, he leads his friends as they run after their passion and strive to prove themselves. In the end, he aims to create a work that belongs to him.
